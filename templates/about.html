About Gym Interface

This innovative application leverages state-of-the-art regression modeling and interactive visualizations to forecast pull-up progress. Designed with modern aesthetics and seamless user experience, Gym Interface offers dynamic data manipulation, insightful analytics, and intuitive navigation.

Our goal is to provide an unparalleled tool for fitness tracking and performance prediction, combining advanced algorithms with a cutting-edge user interface. Explore the dashboard, add or remove data, and view historical trends in a beautifully designed environment.

Join us in revolutionizing fitness analytics!
